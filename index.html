The Moon

A Natural Satellite

The Moon is the only natural satellite of the Earth, and the fifth largest satellite in the Solar System. It is the largest
natural satellite of a planet in the Solar System relative to the size of its primary, having a quarter the diameter of Earth
but only 1/81 its' mass.

Statistics About the Moon

  • Semi-major axis: 384,399 km
  • Orbital period: 27.321582 days
  • Average orbital speed: 1.022 km/s
  • Mean radius: 1,737.10 km
  • Surface area: 3.793 × 1077 km2
  • Volume: 2.1958 × 1010 km3
  • Mass: 7.3477 × 1022kg
